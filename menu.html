Welcome to the Flutter Tutor Booking App

Our app connects students with tutors for personalized one-on-one sessions. Find the perfect tutor for your learning needs and book a session with ease.

Features

  • Search for tutors based on availability and subject expertise.
  • Book sessions directly through the app with a user-friendly interface.
  • Receive notifications for booking confirmations and reminders.
  • Manage bookings and view history easily.

About Us

We are dedicated to making education more accessible by bridging the gap between students and qualified tutors. Our app is designed to simplify the process of finding and booking tutoring sessions.
