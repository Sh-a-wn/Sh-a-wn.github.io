Welcome to Course Correct

Course Correct aims to connect students with tutors for personalized one-on-one sessions. Find the perfect tutor for your learning needs and book a session with ease.

Discover More

Features

  • Search for tutors based on availability and subject expertise.
  • Book sessions directly through the app with a user-friendly interface.
  • Receive reminders for upcoming sessions with tutors.
  • Manage bookings and view history easily.

Meet our Tutors

The tutors on the app range from top-performing students in higher classes to young but experienced individuals eager to assist those who need help.

Students

Anyone can be a student on the app. Just log in as a student and select a topic from our vast options where you need assistance, and we'll get you the best tutor available to guide you.

About Us

We are dedicated to making education more accessible by bridging the gap between students and experienced tutors. Our app is designed to simplify the process of finding and booking tutoring sessions.
