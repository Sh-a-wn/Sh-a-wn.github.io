About Course Correct

Course Correct is designed to make finding and booking tutoring sessions seamless and efficient. Whether you need help with a specific subject or ongoing support, our platform connects you with the best tutors available.

Meet Our Tutors

Our tutors range from top-performing students in higher education to experienced professionals eager to assist. Each tutor is carefully vetted to ensure quality support and expertise.

For Students

As a student, you can easily find a tutor by logging in, selecting the topic you need help with, and scheduling a session. Our user-friendly interface ensures a smooth and straightforward booking process.

About Us

We are committed to enhancing education by connecting students with knowledgeable tutors. Our goal is to make learning accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
